Project for Aerial Recovery

When the 6.9 earthquake struck Central Cebu, I spent a week on the ground documenting the response alongside Aerial Recovery and Global Empowerment Mission.

It wasn’t a paid project. It was something I chose to be part of.

Moving through affected communities, I focused on what couldn’t be measured. The urgency, the quiet strength, and the human side of every moment.

This piece isn’t just about the response. It’s about what it felt like to be there.
